Welcome to School Basic!

School Basic is a project that was conceived and launched in October 2022 after months of careful planning. Our aim was to create an avenue to promote awareness and education in the field of cybersecurity, specifically addressing the challenges and dangers present in the digital world.

We recognized the growing importance of cybersecurity as the internet became an integral part of our daily lives. However, many individuals remain unaware of the risks and lack the knowledge to protect themselves effectively. School Basic was born out of the desire to bridge this knowledge gap and empower people to safeguard their digital presence.

One of our primary objectives is to create a community that encompasses people from all walks of life, regardless of age or background. We believe that cybersecurity is a concern that affects everyone and that unity is essential in facing the vulnerabilities we encounter.

The core value of our project is rooted in helping others acquire knowledge, learn new skills, and defend themselves against the ever-evolving threats in the digital landscape. We aspire to be a supportive platform that fosters learning and collaboration.

Additionally, School Basic recognizes the importance of programming languages and their role in both understanding cybersecurity and developing secure solutions. Our project provides resources, tutorials, and projects to assist individuals who are new to programming or considering starting their journey in this field.

Join us in our mission to build a large, inclusive community that supports and empowers each other. Together, we can navigate the digital dangers and create a safer online environment.
